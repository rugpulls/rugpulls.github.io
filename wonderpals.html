ABOUT

WonderPals is a collection of 10,000 delightfully cute NFTs living on the Ethereum blockchain, created from a combination of over 200 traits with varying rarity across different categories. There will be some special 1/1s sprinkled in the collection, and you might get lucky and mint one with a trait from your favorite project! Our vision is to bring more joy to the world and bring people together through community, creativity, and fun art!

FAQ

What is the supply & mint price?

There will be a total of 10,000 WonderPals available.

Mint price 0.08 ETH.

When is the launch date?

We are launching on February 26, 2022 at 2pm PST / 5pm EST.

NOTE: We will never have a stealth-launch, please be careful of scams. Official launch details will be shared on our website, Discord, and our Twitter page.

What is the utility?

Holders of WonderPals will have a fun & cute piece of art, plus access to our close-knit community, holder-exclusive giveaways, contests, priority in future collections, and more!

What are NFTs?

NFT stands for “non-fungible token” which are unique digital items that are stored and encrypted on the blockchain that people can buy, own, and trade. NFTs can simply be digital art but can have various benefits. They can be treated like membership cards where holders get exclusive access to things like websites, events, merchandise, and more.

How do I buy a WonderPals NFT?

1. Sign up for Metamask or Coinbase Wallet and download the extension on your internet browser.

2. Make sure you have enough Ethereum in your wallet to cover the total cost including gas fees.

3. On mint day, there will be a Connect button at the top of our website to connect to your wallet.

4. Click on the mint button and you will be prompted to sign for your transaction. There will be a fee associated with every transaction related to gas prices.

5. Once you have made your purchase, your WonderPals NFTs will appear in your wallet and on Opensea!

About The Founder

Hello there! My name is Mina, I am the founder & artist behind WonderPals. I am a freelance artist and senior product designer with nearly 10 years of experience in design, e-commerce & marketing, working with companies like Tiffany & Co., Target, Pinterest, and more! My work has always revolved around the development of visual languages & branding with the goal of bringing delightful experiences to everyone. Expressing positivity through my art is what I truly love to do. I have always been a creator of cute characters and calming aesthetics. When I first started designing WonderPals, I wanted to create a lovable character that anyone could identify with no matter their age, gender, or nationality. A WonderPal can be anything it wants to be, and so can you! I hope to continue my journey as an artist through this new project with the support of the amazing community we are building. I have big dreams for WonderPals and I am so thankful for the overwhelming support we have gotten so far! I am excited for what the future holds.
